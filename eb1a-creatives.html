How artists typically qualify.

The EB-1A requires at least 3 of 10 regulatory criteria. For artists with a strong record, Burlington typically identifies the following:

i
Awards and recognition
Grammy nominations, BET, AFRIMA, Headies, MOBO, or other nationally or internationally recognised awards in the performing arts.
iii
Published material about the artist
Features in Rolling Stone, Billboard, Variety, The Guardian, Pulse Nigeria, or equivalent major media.
ix
Commercial success
Streaming numbers, concert revenue, album sales, international distribution deals.
vii
Critical role at a distinguished organisation
Headlining a major festival, leading a record label, or holding a creative director role.
What a green card means

Six practical advantages.

U.S. touring without visa anxiety. No more B-1/B-2 applications, no tour dates at risk.

Direct label and management deals. Work directly with U.S. labels, publishers, and streaming platforms as a permanent resident.

U.S. business formation. Open a U.S. entity, receive payments directly, structure international revenue.

Family sponsorship. Bring your spouse and children as permanent residents.

Global career mobility

No employer ties. Tour, record, and collaborate freely.
