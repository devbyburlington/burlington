Background

Samuel “Sammie” Alifa is a filmmaker and visual storyteller based in Nigeria. He studied Guidance and Counselling at Ahmadu Bello University, Kaduna, and has been creating film and video content since producing his first short film in 2020. His work spans short-form narrative, documentary-style storytelling, and brand content — with a particular focus on bringing authentic African professional stories to screen.

Role at Burlington

Sammie leads Burlington Consult's creative direction — the visual identity, video content, and storytelling that shapes how the firm presents itself to prospective clients. His work covers brand films, client testimonial content, social media creative, and the visual language that connects Burlington's advisory work to the professionals it serves.

He brings a filmmaker's discipline to every piece of content the firm produces: narrative structure, visual consistency, and the understanding that credibility is built not just by what you say, but by how it looks when you say it.

Every client has a story that's bigger than a petition. My job is to make sure the world sees it.
